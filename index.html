Data & Privacy

We don't store your
clients' data. Full stop.

Not a policy footnote. It's how Nodebook is architected from day one. Your client data never touches our servers and is never used to train AI.

Zero data storage

We connect via secure OAuth API. We read, process and push data in real time. Nothing is saved on our servers — ever. Your data lives in your tools, not ours.

✓ Verified architecture

AI is not trained on your data

We use the Claude API by Anthropic. Anthropic does not train their models on API usage data. Your clients' financials are never used to train any AI model.

✓ Anthropic API policy

UK GDPR & ICO compliant

Nodebook is registered with the ICO as a data processor. We sign a full Data Processing Agreement with every client. You are the data controller. Always.

✓ ICO Registered

You are the data controller. We are the data processor. Your client data lives in your Xero, QuickBooks or Sage account. Nodebook is a pipe — it processes in transit and delivers the output. We act as your technology department, not your data custodian.

Common Questions

What if I want to cancel?

All contracts are annual and renew automatically. Give us 60 days notice before your renewal date to cancel. We don't offer monthly rolling — we're a committed partner, not a month-to-month tool.

What tools do you connect to?

Xero, QuickBooks, Sage, FreeAgent, HMRC MTD, Dext, Karbon, TaxCalc, Outlook, Gmail, Slack, DocuSign and more. If you use it, we can likely connect it.

How long does setup take?

30 days from kickoff to go-live. We handle everything — you just need to attend one scoping call and approve the final build.

Is the setup fee refundable?

No — the setup fee covers your custom build. Once we start, the work begins. We stand behind the quality of every system we build.
